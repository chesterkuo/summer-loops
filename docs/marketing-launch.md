## LinkedIn Post

**Introducing Warmly - Your Personal CRM for Professional Relationships**

After months of development, Warmly is now live on the App Store!

### Why You Need a Personal CRM

Enterprise CRM is built for companies. But your professional network is YOUR asset.

Warmly is a Personal CRM designed for individuals who understand that relationships drive opportunities.

**What makes Warmly different from traditional contact apps:**

| Traditional Contact Apps | Warmly Personal CRM |
|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Store contact info only | Record every interaction & conversation |
| Static contact lists | Dynamic relationship strength tracking |
| Search to find people | AI finds the best introduction path |
| Data silos | Visual network mapping |
| Your contacts stay isolated | **Team & Individual Sharing** |

### Core Features:

📇 **AI Business Card Scanner** - Snap a photo, AI extracts everything. Batch mode for networking events.

🎤 **Voice Memos** - Record your thoughts right after meeting someone with live transcription.

🗺️ **Network Universe** - Visualize your entire professional network. Discover hidden connections.

🔍 **Path Discovery** - Want to meet someone? Find the warmest intro path. AI drafts the message for you.

✍️ **Quick Notes** - Describe who you met in natural language. AI organizes it into a contact profile.

📊 **Relationship Tracking** - Know which relationships need nurturing before they go cold.

👥 **Team & Individual Sharing** - Share specific contacts with your team or selectively with individuals. Your network multiplies when you collaborate. Control what you share - keep private notes private.

### Your Network is Your Net Worth

Every handshake, every business card, every conversation is a potential opportunity.

Warmly helps you remember everyone you meet and find the best path to connect when you need it.
